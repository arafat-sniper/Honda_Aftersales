Honda Mission

Maintaining a global viewpoint, we are dedicated to supplying products of the highest quality, yet at a reasonable price for worldwide customer satisfaction.

01
📞
Pre‑Arrival Preparation
Service Advisor + System
  • Prepare workstation using 5S
  • Confirm appointment in advance
  • Review service history & campaigns
  • Pre‑classify job type
  • Verify parts availability
Target: 100% appointments confirmed
Exception: Unreachable → Send WhatsApp → Notify Reception
02
🚗
Reception & Check‑In
Service Advisor
  • Greet the customer within 2 minutes; offer refereshments to customer
  • Tablet check‑in with photos
  • Advise the customer on required service as per service history
  • Record customer concern in detail on receiving form (VOC)
  • Confirm customer concerns to the customer; with QC (if required)
  • Explain the job in detail which will be carried out
  • Inform customer about ongoing campaigns/promotions; offer service package and additives (if applicable)
  • Confirm ETA to customer & promised delivery time; gather customer approval and signature before proceeding (WhatsApp approval if vehicle received by D2D)
  • Apply Priority Sticker and mark vehicle status (Appointment, Express, D2D, Walk-in or Repeat Repairs)
  • Open Job Card and handover to Team Leader; guide the customer to the Customer Lounge
Target: ≤ 10 minutes
Exception: Queue → supervisor support
03
🔧
Vehicle Loading & Job Allocation
Team Leader
  • Review Job Type & Customer concerns
  • Confirm Priority Status: Appointment, Express, D2D, Walk-in or Repeat Repairs
  • Assign vehicle
  • Explain job details to the technician
Target: Bay entry ≤ 15 mins
Exception: Delay → Inform Service Advisor to update Promised Delivery Time
04
⚙️
Service Execution
Technician + Team Leader + QC
  • Carry out required task/service as per checksheet (for PM Service)
  • Notify team leader and Service advisor in case of additional findings along with photos/videos
  • Document findings on the repair order
  • Proceed with additional repairs only after confirmation from team leader/service advisor
  • Quality check to be conducted by Team Leader (PM) or Quality Controller (GR)
Target: First‑time fix
Exception: Safety → stop & escalate
05
📲
Proactive Communication
Service Advisor
  • Status update on vehicle progress and additional findings with proper explanation (if any)
  • Confirm approval for additional repairs and update on job card with customer signature (if customer waiting) or by WhatsApp/telephone
  • Update ETA to customer and on the job card
  • Provide confirmation to team leader on customer approval/feedback
Target: No pending updates
Exception: Customer escalations → inform Service Manager
06
✅
QC & Vehicle Wash
Team Leader + Washing
  • Final inspection to be done by Team Leader/QC
  • Test drive (if applicable)
  • Verify job completion and prepare proforma invoice
  • Send for vehicle washing
  • Team leader notify SA car sent for wash
Target: QC first pass
Exception: QC fail → rework
07
🤝
Active Delivery
Service Advisor
  • Verify job completion, vehicle cleanliness, replaced parts and proforma invoice
  • Inform customer vehicle ready for collection
  • Explain all work carried out along with customer walkaround
  • Show photos/videos of findings and completed repairs (before/after)
  • Explain deferred work (if any); details about next upcoming service and schedule next service after confirming with customer
  • Confirm that all customer concerns are handled; escort the customer to cashier
  • Invite customer to leave a Google review; thank the customer and see off
Target: 100% explanation
Exception: Complaint → recovery
08
📬
Follow‑Up & CSI
Service Advisor + CRM
  • Follow‑up call to be carried out within 24-48 hrs
  • CSI survey sent via WhatsApp
  • Google review link sent via WhatsApp
  • Log customer complaints/feedback in the system
Target: CSI = 100%
Exception: Low CSI or Customer Complaints → escalation to service manager

Service Excellence
Roles & Responsibilities

Clear accountability by role to ensure a consistent customer experience and strong process control.

Service Advisor
Customer Owner
Primary point of contact from booking to delivery & follow-up.
  • Confirm appointments, prepare workload, check history/campaigns, and ensure parts readiness.
  • Perform reception/check-in, capture VOC, take photos, and explain work scope clearly.
  • Open job card, assign priority category, and handover to Team Leader.
  • Proactively update customer on status, additional findings, approvals, and revised ETA.
  • Deliver vehicle with clear explanation, walkaround, and invoice readiness.
  • Complete CSI/follow-up and log feedback/complaints for closure.
Focus: Communication quality, accuracy of VOC, approvals, promised time control.
Team Leader
Flow Controller
Controls workshop flow and allocates jobs for productivity and quality.
  • Review job type, priority status, and customer concerns before allocation.
  • Assign bay/technician and ensure job clarity and correct checklist usage.
  • Support diagnosis for additional findings and coordinate approval path with SA.
  • Ensure process compliance during execution (tools, standards, documentation).
  • Conduct/coordinate QC (PM) and confirm readiness before wash & delivery steps.
  • Escalate constraints (capacity/parts/delays) early to protect promised time.
Focus: Bay entry time, balanced allocation, rework prevention, escalation discipline.
Technician
Execution
Performs diagnosis/repair/service to standard and documents evidence.
  • Execute service/repair as per checklist and repair order instructions.
  • Identify additional findings, capture photos/videos, and report to Team Leader/SA.
  • Document work performed and findings accurately on repair order.
  • Do not proceed with additional work without approval confirmation.
  • Ensure workmanship quality, correct torque/fluids, and clean handover condition.
Focus: First-time fix, proper documentation, approval compliance.
Quality Controller (QC)
Quality Gate
Confirms repair completeness and prevents repeat repairs.
  • Perform final inspection and verify all job items completed.
  • Test drive when applicable and confirm symptom resolution.
  • Validate workmanship, safety checks, and cleanliness readiness for delivery.
  • Record QC results and return for rework if any failure is detected.
  • Support root-cause feedback loop to reduce repeat repairs.
Focus: QC first-pass, safety compliance, repeat-repair reduction.
Workshop Supervisor
Workshop Lead
Leads the workshop team and controls workflow to deliver on-time repairs with the highest quality standards.
  • Supervise and lead the team to perform their daily work according to the process on hand with the highest quality standards.
  • Organize the team utilizing organizational tools and processes which are in place, with ability to improve on them.
  • Motivate the team, ensuring that every member of the team delivering a 100% at all times.
  • Ensure the accuracy of the productivity calculations of the team.
  • Ensure that all work orders are closed and costed in timely manner.
  • Solve any problem encountered during the course of daily work which can be technical or otherwise.
Focus: Workflow control, productivity accuracy, timely costing/closure, technical escalation handling.
Reception Manager
Front Desk Lead
Owns reception operations, customer intake coordination, and D2D logistics readiness.
  • Prepare receiving forms (Vehicle Receiving Form – VRF, Receiving Note) one day before scheduled jobs (N-1).
  • Reconfirm D2D appointments on the morning of service (N-0).
  • Reception/Lead Driver to confirm the customer delivery time based on driver availability and allocated the job.
  • Manage cancellations and rescheduling in coordination with CRM.
  • Provide drivers with delivery notes, invoices, and customer contact details.
  • Track overtime and attendance until a digital system is implemented.
  • Ensure sufficient number of VHC sheets / eVHC and protective materials are available at reception.
Focus: Queue control, documentation accuracy, D2D coordination, stock readiness and customer handover support.
Service Manager
Governance
Owns escalations, KPI governance, and customer recovery decisions.
  • Monitor service KPIs (promised time, CSI, QC pass rate, repeat repairs).
  • Handle customer escalations and authorize recovery actions when needed.
  • Ensure SOP compliance, staffing/capacity planning, and continuous improvement.
  • Review abnormal cases (delays, safety stops, complaint trends) and drive countermeasures.
  • Coach team on communication standards and service excellence behaviors.
Focus: Escalation closure, KPI cadence, SOP adherence, customer recovery.

Key KPIs

Key KPIs by role to monitor performance, quality, and customer experience.

Service Advisor
Customer Owner
  • Sales target achievement % (Service & Parts).
  • NPS / CSI target achievement.
  • Added value units sold (Fuel Treatment, Injector Cleaner, AC Treatment).
  • Tires / Windshield / Washer incentive items volume.
  • Service contract sales count & payout eligibility.
  • New vehicle lead conversion count.
Reception Manager
Front Desk Lead
  • Appointments confirmed (target: 100%).
  • Customer greeted / attended time.
  • Check‑in completed within target time.

  • D2D reconfirmation completion (N‑0).
  • VHC/eVHC & protective materials availability.
Team Leader
Flow Controller
  • Bay entry time (target: ≤ 15 mins).
  • Team Leader QCI / inspection ratio (% of throughput).
  • Team productivity % and technician utilization %.

  • Open WIP ageing / bottleneck resolution.

Execution

  • Utilization.
  • Productivity.

  • First‑time fix rate (reduce repeat repairs).
  • Documentation completeness (photos/remarks/checklists).

Quality Gate

  • Repeat repair / comeback reduction.
  • QCI inspection ratio tracking.
  • FRFT contribution (fix it right first time).
  • Safety compliance (roadworthy delivery).
Workshop Supervisor
Workshop Lead
  • Team productivity calculations accuracy.
  • Technician utilization and productivity targets.
  • Work orders closed & costed on time.
  • QCI inspection ratio improvement.
  • Repeat repair reduction through coaching.

Governance
  • Service revenue achievement %.
  • CSI Top Box and NPS targets.
  • FRFT target achievement.
